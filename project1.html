Authentic Portraits

Capturing the essence of Kenyan youth

The Story

This portrait series explores the authentic spirit of Kenya's young generation. Each photograph captures not just a face, but a story, a dream, and the unwavering ambition that defines our youth. Shot in natural lighting around Eldoret, these portraits showcase the beauty, strength, and diversity of our community.

Working with local talent, we created a safe space where personalities could shine through, resulting in images that speak to the soul and celebrate individuality. Each portrait is a testament to the belief that ambition truly is priceless.

Project Details

Category: Portrait Photography
